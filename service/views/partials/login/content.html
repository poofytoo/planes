What is Planes?

Planes is a silly little game that puts your code to the test against your friend's code! Challenge them in an epic duel of planes!

view sample game rules and documentation

How does it work?

Making your AI is easy! Each round, you must return a plane action- one of up, down, charge, or shoot. You get information such as the current gameRound and the position of your enemy. Check out the rules and documentation!
